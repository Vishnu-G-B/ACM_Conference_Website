    Neural Networks, Deep Learning, Data Mining, Big Data Analytics, Explainable AI, Statistical Inference, Data Visualization

  2. Industrial Application

    Circuits, Reliability, and Fault Tolerance, Computer-Aided Design and Verification, Digital Circuits and FPGA based Designs, Emerging and Post-CMOS Technologies, System Design and Security, VLSI for Applied and Future Computing, Systems & Control

  3. IoT, Security, Cloud Computing, Networks

    Wireless Sensor Networks, Vehicular Communication Networks, 4G/5G/Next-generation Network Evolutions, Mobile and Wireless Communication, Optical communications, RF and microwave communications, Green Communications, IoT Architectures and protocols, QoS Management in IoT Networks, IoT in 5G Networks, Smart Grids and Microgrids, Cloud and IoT Networks, Information Theory and Coding, Cryptology, Blockchain technology, Security Protocols, Privacy-enhancing technologies, Viruses and Malware

    Signal processing, 2D/3D Object Detection and Recognition, Image and Video Analysis, Document Image Processing, Audio and Acoustic Processing, Image, Video Coding and Compression, Human-Computer Interaction, Digital Topology and Geometry, Biometrics, Multimedia Technologies

  5. Computational Biology

    DNA computing, Genetic algorithm, Artificial intelligence tools & applications, Knowledge discovery, Data mining, Knowledge management, Visual information processing, Artificial fish swarm algorithm, Hybrid intelligent algorithm, Simulated annealing algorithm, Particle swarm algorithm, Immune algorithm, Neural networks, Machine learning, Visual information processing, AI applied to the IoT (Internet of Things), Health big data, Intelligent manufacturing.

    Mobile computing, and distributed systems; computer systems with a focus on architecture, parallel processing, and dependability; embedded systems; signal, image, and speech processing; software engineering; security and database systems; human-computer interaction; vision and graphics; programming languages; theory of computing; information systems; information technology; big data technologies and their applications; mobile information systems; web intelligence applications and search; the study of recent trends in information technology; computing in the context of the internet of things; leveraging machine learning for the internet of everything; and finally, information retrieval.

Instruction for Authors

Submission Format

Prepare your submission as a single PDF document and submit it through the CMT3 conference management system, accessible here: cmt3.research.microsoft.com/CODEAI2024.

Document Templates

Craft your paper in line with the designated style by making use of the Word or LaTeX templates available through the Taylor&Francis Template. If LaTeX resonates with you, the "CODE:AI" proceedings template is at your disposal. Word enthusiasts, you're welcome to adopt the provided template, ensuring that your ultimate submission takes the form of a unified PDF.

Originality's Embrace

Embody the spirit of innovation. Your submission should be original, devoid of previous publication in conferences or journals, and not currently under review elsewhere. Our commitment to this essence is upheld by utilizing a plagiarism detection software service, iThenticate, copyscape, plagscan, etc.

A Touch of Anonymity

During the review process, your submission shall embark on a journey of double anonymity. Reviewers will engage with your work without knowledge of your identity. If you choose, you may fill-in your name with your manuscript. We embrace both the anonymous and known submissions.

Pages as Canvas

Craft your narrative with finesse, within the boundaries of six pages for papers and four pages for short papers. These limits encompass your references, urging you to paint your thoughts with precision.

Legacy in a renowned Journal - Taylor and Francis

Works that grace the threshold of acceptance shall etch their mark in perpetuity within the esteemed Taylor&Francis Journal. There, they shall stand as beacons of knowledge, accessible to curious minds worldwide.
